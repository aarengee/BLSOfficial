Brightlands School (or BLS) is a private school located in the Dalanwala suburb of Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. Brightlands made a modest beginning in 1958 as a co-educational prepatory school.Today it is a premier institution upholding its mandate of being a premier 10+2 institution firmly committed to providing quality education both in academics and transferrable skills

Mr Ravi Narang is the director of our elite school. Every year 12th August is a holiday at the school for Mrs. Narang's Memorial Day.
