Brightlands School (or BLS) is a private school located in the Dalanwala suburb of Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. Brightlands was established in 1958 by Mrs. Narang as a school for primary students. It is now a 10+2 senior-secondary level, English medium, co-educational school affiliated to the ICSE Council, New Delhi.

After Mrs Narangs demise, Mr. Ravi Narang (her son) became the director. 12th August is a holiday at the school for Mrs. Narang's Memorial Day.
