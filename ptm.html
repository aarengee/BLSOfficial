Social Service

Social Service plays a significant role in the extra curricular activities that are undertaken by the students.

Each student is required to complete a certain quota of Socially Useful Productive Work: S.U.P.W.

Counselling

As part of the faculty, a proffesional counsellor is available to the students during and after school hours. Group sessions,individual counselling as well as career guidance through seminars and aptitude tests are conducted frequently.

The counsellor has regular interaction with the faculty and parents in a supporting role such that the students always have "someone to turn to"


Parent Teacher Meetings

Parent -Teacher meetings are of vital importance. This proactive interaction enables parents and teachers to have a better understanding of their child's total environment and development. PTM's are held at regular intervals where parents can discuss with the concerned staff the progress and problems of the child
